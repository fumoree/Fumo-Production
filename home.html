In an autumn forest, the main character confronts a pursuing doppelgänger—symbolizing his inner fears and self-destruction. As he tries to escape himself, he becomes increasingly lost, until he ultimately faces his reflection and kills it. This serves as an allegory for inner conflict and self-destruction.
